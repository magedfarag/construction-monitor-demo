# ADR-001 — Free/public and Middle East-first scope

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The project scope spans construction monitoring, imagery, maritime, aviation, and contextual events. The source inventory shows large variation in cost, Middle East suitability, and licensing posture.

## Decision
The initial platform will prefer free/public and Middle East-capable sources first. Commercial sources remain future escalation paths.

## Consequences
- MVP expectations must be bounded.
- Public-source limitations must be explicit.
- Upgrade seams for premium providers must exist from the start.
